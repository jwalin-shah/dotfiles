# Research

Multi-source deep research: fan out to find sources, adversarially verify claims, synthesize a cited report.

## Process

1. Clarify the research question
2. Fan out: multiple search angles, different sources
3. Verify: each claim checked against its source
4. Synthesize: findings merged into a single report
5. Cite: every claim has a source

## Output

A research report with: Question, Sources consulted, Findings (verified vs. plausible), Open questions.
